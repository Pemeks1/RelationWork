Measurement Scales

Understand and Track Emotional Impact

Use the following scales to rate each topic after reflection. You can apply them after individual and couple discussions.

A. My Personal Impact (1–5)
  • 1 – Very Negative / Draining
  • 2 – Slightly Negative / Challenging
  • 3 – Neutral / Mixed
  • 4 – Slightly Positive / Nourishing
  • 5 – Very Positive / Empowering
B. My Partner’s Perceived Impact (1–5)
  • 1 – Very Negative for them
  • 2 – Slightly Negative / Challenging for them
  • 3 – Neutral / Mixed for them
  • 4 – Slightly Positive for them
  • 5 – Very Positive for them
C. Our Relationship’s Health (1–5)
  • 1 – Actively Damaging
  • 2 – Creates Significant Strain
  • 3 – Stable / Neutral
  • 4 – Builds Connection
  • 5 – Core Strength & Joy
